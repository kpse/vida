About N interior

关于集恩

N interior Design Company specialises in Architecture Planning, Interior Design, Lighting Design, Space Decoration and all related client requirements. We collaborate with international architects, experts and design studios to best service our clients' needs. We believe that outstanding design is the key to a beautiful life.

集恩空间设计汇集国内领先设计师与团队, 集合建筑规划、 室内设计、灯光设计、陈设艺术、产品代购、定制施工等项目服务, 联合国内外知名设计师与合作所, 为不同需求的客户提供商业与私宅的国际领先设计与施工方案。
